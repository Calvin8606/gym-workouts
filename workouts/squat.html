The Art of Squats

Desciption: The squat is a very important exercise as it works the posterior chain. By training this movement, not only does it grow your low body but it also works your core.

Rules to having a successful squat:

  • Feet should be just outside shoulder width
  • Bar should be on top of your traps
  • Take a deep breath in and brace your core
  • Control the weight down
  • Make sure to lean foward when going down but do not arch your back
  • Hips should be perpindicular to your knee when down
  • PUSH THE WEIGHT UP!!! (Tip: Imagine you are pushing against the floor)

Steps:

  1. Place bar on traps
  2. Get the right feet positioning
  3. Take a deep breath and brace your core
  4. Control the weight down
  5. Stop when your hips are perpindicular to your knees
  6. Press it UP!!!!!!
